Module inprod_analytic

Inner products between the truncated set of basis functions for the ocean and atmosphere streamfunction fields.

These are partly calculated using the analytical expressions from Cehelsky, P., & Tung, K. K. 87). Theories of multiple equilibria and weather regimes-A critical reexamination. Part II: Baroclinic two-layer models. Journal of the atmospheric sciences, 44(21), 3282-3303, 1987.
